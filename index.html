How To Play

Step 1: Click Start! If you have a previously saved gamed, clicking start will load it.
Step 2: Guess and select a letter
Step 3: Wait to see if the letter you guessed is part of the word, if not watch your hang man grow!
Step 4: Guess a different letter until your word is complete
Step 5: See if you win or loose!
Step 6: Click End Game and then Let's Play to start a new game!
